Guanyao Li (李冠耀)

 
 
 

About me


I am currently a senior researcher at Guangzhou Urban Planning and Design Survey Research Institute and a postdoctoral researcher at South China University of Technology. I was selected in the National Overseas Postdoctoral Talent Recruitment Programs (国家海外博士后人才引进计划) in 2023.


In 2022, I received my Ph.D degree at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) (Advisor: Prof. Shueng-Han Gary Chan). Previously, I received my Master degree in Computer Science from National Chiao Tung University (Advisor: Prof. Wen-Chih Peng), and my B.Eng. in Computer Science from Zhengjiang University (Advisor: Prof. Ling Chen). I have been a member of Zhejiang University Morningside Cultural China Scholar Program since 2014.


I enjoy conducting applied research on real data and solving real problems. My research interest lies in the fields of data mining and deep learning, with a special focus on spatial-temporal data mining for user analytics, smart transportation, urban planning, and smart city using deep learning techniques. I am a member of ACM.
