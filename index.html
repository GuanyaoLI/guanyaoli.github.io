About me


I am currently a PhD student at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), under the supervision of Prof. Shueng-Han Gary Chan. In 2017, I received my Master degree in Computer Science from National Chiao Tung University under the supervision of Prof. Wen-Chih Peng. Previously, I received my B.Eng.in Computer Science from Zhengjiang University in 2015.


I enjoy conducting applied research on real data and sovling real problems. My research interest lies in the fields of data mining and deep learning, with a special focus on spatial-temporal data mining for user analytics, smart transportation, and smart city using deep learning techniques.
